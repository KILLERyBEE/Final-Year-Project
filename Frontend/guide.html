Explore how to use AirNav's gesture-driven modules. Master each feature, gesture, and shortcut to take full control of your digital world.

Main Program

The main program serves as the central hub for all gesture-controlled modules. It initialises the webcam, loads the gesture recognition model, and delegates control to the appropriate module based on the detected gesture mode.

Gesture Shortcuts

  • All Fingers Up – Activate Scroll Mode
  • Index Finger Up – Activate Zoom Mode
  • Index and Pinky Finger Up – Activate Volume Mode
  • Index, Middle and Ring Finger Up – Activate Video Mode
  • Index and Thumb Finger Up – Activate Document Mode
  • Index and Middle Finger Up – Slide Travel
  • Middle and Index and Pinky Finger Up – Screenshot Mode
  • Pinky and Thumb Finger Up – AppSwitching Mode

Scroll Control

The scroll module tracks the orientation and position of your index finger to scroll web pages and documents naturally. It supports velocity-sensitive scrolling for both fine-control and rapid navigation across long pages.

Try Now →

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Index Finger Up – Scroll Up
  • Index and Middle Finger Up – Scroll Down
  • Thumb Up – Fast Scroll Up
  • All Fingers Up – Fast Scroll Down

Zoom Control

Zoom in and out of your screen or applications with a simple pinch gesture. The module intelligently detects the distance between your thumb and index finger, translating finger spacing into smooth, lag-free zoom transitions in real-time.

Try Now →

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Index and Middle Finger Up – Zoom Out
  • Index Finger Up – Zoom In

Volume Adjustment

Control system audio intuitively with vertical hand gestures. AirNav maps the vertical position of your palm to the system audio level in real-time, supporting both smooth gradual changes and quick mute toggles via a fist gesture.

Try Now →

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Index Finger Up – Increase Volume
  • Index and Middle Finger Up – Decrease Volume

Video Playback Control

AirNav supports media controls including play, pause, fast-forward, and rewind — all through mid-air gestures. A closed fist pauses playback, while pointing sideways seeks forward or backward. Works universally with any video application.

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Pinch- Open a Video
  • Fist – Close a Video
  • All Fingers Up – Play/Pause

App Switching

Switch between open applications seamlessly using hand gestures. App switching detects directional swipes and activates the OS-level Alt+Tab functionality, allowing you to navigate your taskbar without touching a single key.

Try Now →

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Finger to Right – Next App
  • Finger to Left – Previous App

Document Control

Navigate through documents and files using intuitive gestures. Swipe, scroll, zoom, and flip through pages without touching a single key. AirNav recognises open-palm swipes for page turns and pinch gestures for zooming.

Try Now →

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Pinch – Select and Open
  • Index and Middle Finger Up – Previous Page

Slide Travel

Slide travel allows you to control high-res slide presentations from a distance, by simply gesturing left or right to advance or reverse slides. The system also supports a laser-pointer simulation via tracked hand position for emphasis during presenting.

Gesture Shortcuts

  • Index and Middle Finger Up – Previous Slide
